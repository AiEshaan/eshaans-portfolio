# Eshaan Portfolio PRD

## Vision

Create a cinematic, scroll-driven, interactive portfolio that tells the story of Eshaan Mayekar through immersive 3D environments, realistic character interactions, and project storytelling.

The experience should feel like a documentary rather than a traditional portfolio website.

---

## Objectives

* Showcase projects
* Showcase professional experience
* Showcase skills
* Showcase education
* Showcase certifications
* Showcase contact information

while maintaining a single continuous story.

---

## Target Audience

* Recruiters
* Hiring Managers
* Startup Founders
* Technical Leads
* Product Teams

---

## Core Experience

The user explores the portfolio by scrolling through a 3D journey.

The portfolio revolves around a realistic 3D representation of Eshaan.

Every section is physically connected to the character.

---

## Design Philosophy

Human First

Cinematic

Minimal

Professional

Elegant

Timeless

No cyberpunk aesthetics.

No AI cliché visuals.

No excessive neon effects.

No futuristic hologram overload.

---

## Success Metrics

* Recruiters spend more than 3 minutes exploring
* Resume downloads
* LinkedIn visits
* Contact inquiries
* Project exploration rate
