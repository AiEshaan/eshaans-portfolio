# Animation Guidelines

## Core Philosophy

The portfolio should feel like:

* Netflix Documentary
* Apple Product Film
* Architectural Visualization
* Human Storytelling

NOT:

* Cyberpunk
* AI Holograms
* Neon Interfaces
* Sci-Fi Dashboards
* Iron Man UI

---

## Motion Principles

Slow

Intentional

Cinematic

Smooth

Natural

---

## Camera Rules

Camera should behave like a professional cinematographer.

No sudden movements.

No rapid spinning.

No gaming camera effects.

No aggressive zooming.

Use:

* Dolly
* Push In
* Pull Out
* Orbit
* Focus Shift

---

## Lighting Rules

Soft lighting

Natural shadows

Warm highlights

Documentary style

No RGB lighting

No neon lighting

No glowing blue AI effects

---

## Materials

Fabric

Paper

Metal

Glass

Wood

Leather

Avoid futuristic materials.

---

## Scroll Behavior

Each scroll must advance the story.

Every animation must have meaning.

No decorative animations.

---

## Character Rules

Human first.

Technology is part of the journey.

Technology is not the identity.

---

## Section Rules

Projects = Pocket

Experience = Hoodie Vault

Skills = Palm Galaxy

Education = ID Card

Certifications = Archive Folder

Contact = Tablet

Ending = Full Character Reveal
